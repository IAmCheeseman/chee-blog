Red Nivis is a roguelike metroidvania where you explore the ruins of the Red Nivis Laboratory.

5 Unique Areas

There are 5 areas to explore: Lab, Deep Labs, Freezers, Chem Sector, Tech Sector. All of these being unique and focused on a different way to change the gameplay. The Freezer's aquatic based enemies make you play differently than the poison based Chem Sector enemies.

36 Cool Weapons

There are tons of weapons for you to choose from. Weapons build off previous weapons and make them better and better while also making each iteration of the idea different from the last.

A Cast of Awesome NPCs

You have a bunch of unique NPCs which can change the way you play or make the world a better place to play in!

Great Worlds to Explore

You'll have a fun world to play in that captures the feeling that metroidvanias give, while also being your own world. You have a ton of NPCs, weapons, and bosses to find in this weird shape shifting laboratory!
